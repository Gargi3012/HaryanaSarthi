About HaryanaSarthi

Empowering the citizens of Haryana with a centralized, AI-driven platform for discovering and accessing government opportunities.

Our Mission

HaryanaSarthi is an AI-powered digital platform designed to help citizens of Haryana easily discover and access government opportunities. Currently, government opportunities are scattered across multiple portals, making it difficult for citizens to identify the opportunities they are eligible for. We solve this problem by creating a centralized intelligent platform that uses AI and machine learning to match users with the most relevant government opportunities based on their profile.

Our Ultimate Goal: To ensure that no citizen misses a government opportunity due to lack of awareness or accessibility.

Core Platform Features

How our AI-powered intelligence makes discovery easier.

  • AI Eligibility Checker: Automatically analyzes your profile (Age, Education, Category, Family ID, etc.) to determine exactly what you're eligible for.
  • ML Recommendation Engine: Uses vector similarity to rank opportunities into Highly Recommended, Recommended, or Partially Eligible.
  • Multilingual AI Chatbot: A Gemini API powered assistant ready to answer questions in English, Hindi, and Hinglish.
  • Next Best Action AI: Step-by-step guidance on how to apply and what documents are required.
  • Career & Life Event AI: Suggestions tailored to your exact life situation (Student, Farmer, Entrepreneur) and career pathways.
  • Document Readiness AI: Pre-checks requirement catalogs to prevent application rejections due to missing documents.
  • Privacy-First Security: Utilizes Family ID Tokenization to ensure citizen data remains totally secure.
  • Smart Alerts: Real-time notifications for scheme deadlines, exam registrations, and new internships.
