9. Key Observations & Recommendations

Observations: Data indicates that scholarships drive the highest engagement among students (45% of total traffic), whereas government schemes have a relatively low exploration rate.

Recommendations:

  • Improve discoverability of State Schemes by linking them directly to student profiles based on income parameters.
  • Enhance mobile-first outreach in Nuh and Fatehabad where current platform penetration is below 5%.
  • Promote agricultural initiatives which currently sit at the bottom of the "Explored Opportunities" list.
